A nice lockdown project has been rebuilding my fixed gear bike, not that there is anywhere to go on it. The hardest part of this 'rebuild' was freeing a very stuck seatpost.


Part list:

Frame Fort Track.7005, size: diddy (51cm?)
Wheels Ebay specials, 2008
Saddle Unbranded, but blue
Finishing kit Cheapo carbon, slammed
Chainset Unbranded, 2 bolts missing
Chain KMC
Tyres Vittoria Corsa 23c

Gear ratios of 48x18 or 48x16 thanks to a flipflop hub. As I have always lived somewhere with at least some hills the 16 was only ever used on the track. Now I can go back to being one of the cool kids.
