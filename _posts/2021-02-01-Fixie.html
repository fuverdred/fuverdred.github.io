A nice lockdown project has been rebuilding my fixed gear bike, not that there is anywhere to go on it.


Going to a fancy school near Herne Hill Velodrome meant PE could be in the form of track cycling. Inspired by the local hipsters I put together a beautiful blue machine and commuted to school on it every day, as well as racing on the track once/twice a week. However, when I went away to university it was left behind in the shed, and the aluminium seat post and aluminium frame all but became one piece.
